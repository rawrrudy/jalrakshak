# JalRakshak

JalRakshak is a smart water monitoring and management system designed to improve water safety, sustainability, and accessibility through real time environmental intelligence.

The system combines IoT sensors, embedded hardware, and analytics to monitor water quality, detect anomalies, and provide predictive insights for smarter water management.

## Problem

Millions of people around the world face issues related to unsafe water, contamination, poor monitoring infrastructure, and inefficient water usage.

Traditional water testing methods are often expensive, slow, and inaccessible for remote or underserved regions.

JalRakshak aims to provide an affordable, scalable, and intelligent solution for continuous water monitoring.

## Features

- Real time water quality monitoring
- AI based anomaly detection
- Water contamination risk alerts
- IoT enabled remote monitoring
- Cloud dashboard and analytics
- Scalable monitoring network
- Low power embedded hardware
- Future support for solar powered deployment

## Hardware Components
